What is Wipperzite?

Download Video: Closed Format: "MP4" Open Format: "Ogg"

What if there was a place where your could pitch your ideas and see if they are worth the time, money and effort it will take to get them done?

Wipperzeit is a community to do just that. Here you can share your concept, get feedback from the community and learn how to better tell the story of your idea.
