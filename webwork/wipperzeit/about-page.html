About Wiperzeit

A concept incubator for game developers:

Wipperzeit is a community for gamers, developers, and storytellers to share seed stage game concepts with each other and validate and refine those ideas before a single line of code is written.

Quick, agile and proven:

The process is based on established concept generation practices from the game, film and business industries with the end result being a polished set of early-stage marketing materials you can take to investors as well as a blueprint for moving forward in your games development.

FAQs

What will I get out of it?

Wipperzeit guides you through a process of submission and revision with the goal of completing three industry standard deliverables:

  • A High Concept
  • 30 Second Pitch
  • Rocket Pitch

Each of these documents can be used to sell your game to publishers, attract needed team members, or promote a fund-raising campaign for your project.
